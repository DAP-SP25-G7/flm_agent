|           Description: | Course introduction: Scientific socialism is one of the three parts of Marxism-Leninism. Scientific socialism is based on the philosophical methodology of dialectical materialism and historical materialism as well as scientific theoretical foundations of economic laws and economic relations to scientifically explain the advent of the socialist revolution, the formation and development of the communist socio-economic form, associated with the historical mission of the working class, to liberate people and society.  Knowledge: Students will acquire the fundamental and core knowledge of Scientific Socialism, one of the three components of Marxism–Leninism. Skills: Students will enhance their capacity to understand practical realities and apply the knowledge of Scientific Socialism in analyzing and evaluating political and social issues of the country related to socialism and the path toward socialism in Vietnam. Attitude: Students will develop a correct political attitude and ideology toward the subject of Scientific Socialism in particular, and the ideological foundation of the Communist Party in general. To develop critical thinking, reasoning competence, and the ability to apply theories to personal and social life. To emphasize civic roles and social responsibility. Content of the course: The course includes 7 chapters: Chapter 1 presents basic introductory issues of Scientific Socialism (the advent and development of Scientific Socialism); Chapter 2 to Chapter 7 present the basic knowledge of Scientific Socialism to clarify the objective laws of the socialist revolution process. Teaching methods: Lecturing combined with slide presentations, and applying AI to support the learning process. Providing instructions and assigning group projects. Organizing and guiding students in group discussions, presentations, and the creation of creative products. |

## Learning outcomes

| CLO Name | CLO Details | LO Details                                                                                                 |
|----------|-------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1        | CLO1        | Pre-process data and set up a pre-trained neural network.                                                  |
| 2        | CLO2        | Evaluate models by accuracy, specificity, predictive value, confidence intervals, ROC curve, and F1 score. |
| 3        | CLO3        | Diagnose diseases from x-rays and 3D MRI brain images.                                                     |
| 4        | CLO4        | Walk through examples of prognostic tasks.                                                                 |
| 5        | CLO5        | Predict patient survival rates more accurately using tree-based models.                                    |
| 6        | CLO6        | Navigate practical challenges in medicine, like missing data.                                              |
| 7        | CLO7        | Estimate treatment effects on patients using data from randomized trials.                                  |
| 8        | CLO8        | Explore methods to interpret diagnostic and prognostic models.                                             |
| 9        | CLO9        | Automate the task of labeling medical datasets using natural language processing.                          |

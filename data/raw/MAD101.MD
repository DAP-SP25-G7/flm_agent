LO2.3 Determine if a given rule represents a function; perform operations on functions and manipulate functions inlvolving the floor and the ceiling functions. LO2.5 Find a formula for a sequence; evaluate a finite sum with a possible use of special summations.                                         |

LO4.3 Check if a number is prime and find prime factorization of an integer; find greatest common divisor and least common multiple.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |

LO7.3 Check connectivity of a graph and identify its connected components; count paths in a graph. LO7.4 Determine if a graph has Euler/Hamilton paths/circuits; find Euler and Hamilton paths/circuits in a graph.

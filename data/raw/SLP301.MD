|           Description: | This speech processing course comprehensively explains how humans and machines generate, transmit, and process speech signals. The course covers speech production, phonetics, acoustic analysis, speech perception, speech recognition, natural language processing, and speech synthesis. Students will learn about the principles and algorithms behind various speech-processing techniques, including signal processing, machine learning, and deep learning. The course also covers speech processing applications in speech recognition, speech recognition, speech synthesis, and speech enhancement. Upon completing the course, students will have a solid foundation in speech processing and be equipped with the necessary skills to tackle real-world speech-processing problems. |

## 5 LO(s)
| CLO Name | CLO Details | LO Details                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|----------|-------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1        | CLO1        | Gain a comprehensive understanding of fundamental principles in processing speech signals, covering digital signal processing (DSP) methods, feature extraction, and speech signal analysis                                                                 |
| 2        | CLO2        | Develop expertise in speech recognition and synthesis, exploring and applying various algorithms for automatic speech recognition (ASR) and speech synthesis, including rule-based and statistical approaches                                               |

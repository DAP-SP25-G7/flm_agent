|           Description: | 1. Knowledge: - Understand specific characteristics of the historical development and structure of Dan Nhi. - Familiarize students with music theory and basic techniques of Dan Nhi (right-hand bowing and left-hand fingers 1, 2, 3). 2. Skills: - Be able to play 5 songs including 1 foreign song, 2 children's songs, 1 folk song, and 1 Hue-style song while properly applying basic techniques. Students can perform the Hue and folk songs in ensemble. - Course conducted in class units of approximately 15 students. During the course, students will learn: - Historical development of Dan Nhi in Vietnam and other countries (China, Korea, Japan). - Structure and characteristics of Dan Nhi. - Playing posture. - Music theory and basic techniques (right-hand bowing and left-hand pressing with fingers 1, 2, 3). - Play 4 Vietnamese songs: Inh la oi, Vao rung hoa, Ly cay da, Xang xe. - Play 1 foreign song: "Khat vong mua xuan" or Happy Birthday. * Apply IT in the course: a) Provide online resources: web addresses, traditional music clips. b) Use of online resources: lecturers selectively provide resources and guide students in finding information. * Soft skills & Personal development: - Improve perseverance and confidence. - Develop teamwork and independent work skills through assigned tasks. |

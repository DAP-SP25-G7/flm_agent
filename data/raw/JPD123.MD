|           Description: | I. Orientation requirements for course implementation: The course provides basic knowledge and skills of Japanese at elementary level 1 (equivalent to A1.2 level) for students learning Japanese as a second foreign language at University. This course is the follow-up module to JPD113. II. Course Objectives: After completing this course, students can: 1. Introduce their place of origin: such as characteristics of their hometown, food, climate, and famous places. 2. Talk to friends about plans or regular tasks. 3. Invite or suggest friends to participate in an activity; exchange information and discuss to agree on a location and appointment schedule. 4. Simply convey the surrounding situation to others. Additionally, can ask for favors or propose doing something together. 5. Master nearly 300 basic vocabulary words, the writing and reading of more than 40 Kanji characters, and nearly 30 basic grammar structures used in elementary conversation. 6. Practice teamwork skills and self-development through in-class group activities (role play...) and extracurricular activities. |

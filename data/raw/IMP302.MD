|           Description: | Image & Video Processing is an advanced course that covers the fundamental principles, techniques, and applications of image and video processing. The course explores manipulating digital images and videos to extract useful information, enhance visual quality, and perform analysis. It covers various topics, including image acquisition and digitization, image enhancement and restoration, image compression and coding, image segmentation and feature extraction, and object recognition and tracking. The course also covers fundamental concepts in video processing, including video coding and compression, motion estimation, and video analysis. Students will learn to process images and videos using various tools and software platforms, including MATLAB and Python. |

## Learning outcomes

| CLO Name | CLO Details | LO Details                                                                                                                                                    |
|----------|-------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1        | CLO1        | Gain a comprehensive understanding of fundamental principles in image and video processing, digital signal processing in time domain and frequency domain     |
| 2        | CLO2        | Develop expertise in applying the tradditional and machine learning-based techniques for image and video acquisition, digitization, enhancement, restoration. |
| 3        | CLO3        | Develop expertise in applying traditional and machine learning based methods to compress images and video                                                     |
| 4        | CLO4        | Acquire practical skills by using and implementing traditional and machine learning based tools to segment and extract features of an image                   |
| 5        | CLO5        | Develop expertise in applying traditional and machine learning based methods in object detection, motion estimation and video analysis.                       |

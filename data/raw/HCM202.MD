|           Description: | Course introduction: Ho Chi Minh ideology is the crystallization of the thousand-year-old traditions of national construction and defense of the Vietnamese people. On that basis, Ho Chi Minh ideology has filtered the intellectual seeds of the East and the West, creatively applying and developing Marxism-Leninism into the specific conditions of our country. From a philosophical perspective, Ho Chi Minh Thought is a comprehensive and profound system of views on the fundamental issues of the Vietnamese revolution. The goal of Ho Chi Minh Thought is to move toward class liberation, national liberation, and human liberation. Therefore, Ho Chi Minh Thought has become an invaluable spiritual asset and the banner leading the Vietnamese revolution from one victory to another.  Course Objectives: - Knowledge: Students understand the basic knowledge of concepts, origins, formation, and development of Ho Chi Minh Thought; the core contents of Ho Chi Minh Thought; and its application by the Communist Party of Vietnam in the people’s democratic revolution and in the socialist revolution. - Skills: Students develop the ability for independent thinking, analysis, evaluation, and the creative application of Ho Chi Minh Thought to solve practical problems in daily life, academic work, and professional activities. - Attitudes: Students strengthen their political courage and patriotism, remaining loyal to the goal and ideal of national independence combined with socialism; recognize the role and value of Ho Chi Minh Thought for the Party and the Vietnamese nation; and understand their personal responsibility in learning and self-improvement to contribute to national construction and defense. Content of the course: The module consists of 6 chapters providing students with basic knowledge of the Object, research methods, and meaningful learning of the course Ho Chi Minh ideology; the basis and the process of formation and development of Ho Chi Minh ideology; national independence associated with socialism; the Communist Party and State of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam; the great unity bloc with international solidarity; and culture, ethics, and people. Teaching method: Giving lectures combined with slide presentations. Guiding and assigning group work assignments. Organizing and guiding students in group discussions, presentations, and the development of creative products (referencing and applying AI software in a responsible manner). |

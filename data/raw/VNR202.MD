|           Description: | Course introduction: The History of the Communist Party of Vietnam (CPV) is a major and a division of historical science. President Ho Chi Minh affirmed that the history of the CPV is a "phoenix history written in gold." This reflects the scientific, revolutionary, and profound practical value in the Party's platforms and guidelines; it showcases proper leadership and timely responses to requirements set by history, as well as the theoretical lessons of the Vietnamese revolution and the glorious traditions of the Party. Studying this history requires not only mastering historical milestones but also understanding the rich issues in the process of leadership and struggle to apply them to the current period of comprehensive renovation, industrialization, modernization, and international integration.  Course Objectives: - Knowledge: Students will understand basic and systematic knowledge of the establishment of the CPV (1920–1930); the Party’s leadership in the struggle for power (1930–1945); the Party’s leadership in the two wars against French colonialism and U.S. imperialism (1945–1975); and the Party’s leadership in the national transition to socialism and the Doi Moi reforms (1975–2018). - Attitudes: Through historical events and experiences of leadership, students will develop respect for objective truth, and deepen pride and confidence in the Party’s leadership in the past and present. - Skills: Students will develop scientific thinking about history, skills in selecting research materials, and the ability to apply historical knowledge in practice while critically evaluating misconceptions about the Party’s history.  Course Content: This course provides students with basic knowledge of the objects, purposes, tasks, and research methods of the History of CPV. It covers the systematic knowledge of the Party establishment (1920 - 1930) and the process of leading the Vietnamese revolution from 1930 to the present, affirming successes while identifying limitations and experiences. The content includes 3 main chapters: - Chapter 1: The CPV was founded and led the rise to power (1930-1945); - Chapter II: The Party led two resistance wars, completing national liberation and reunification (1945 - 1975); - Chapter III: The Party led the national transition to socialism and carried out the Doi Moi reforms (1975-2018). Through this, students summarize experiences to enhance awareness and develop the ability to apply knowledge to practical work, contributing to the defense of a socialist Vietnam.  Teaching Method: Lectures combined with slide presentations. Guiding group-work assignments. Organizing group discussions, presentations, and creative products (with responsible and ethical use of AI tools). |

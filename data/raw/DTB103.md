|           Description: | 1. Knowledge: - Understand specific characteristics of the historical development and structure of Dan Ty Ba. - Familiarize students with music theory and basic techniques of Dan Ty Ba. - Learn about the ancient music notation system (Hò xự xang...). 2. Skills: - Be able to play 4 Vietnamese songs and 1 common foreign song while properly applying basic techniques. - The course will be conducted in class units of approximately 15 students. During the course, students will learn: - Historical development of the Dan Ty Ba in Vietnam. - Structure and characteristics of the Dan Ty Ba. - Playing posture. - Music theory and basic techniques, including: + Right-hand plucking to create sound combined with left-hand pressing to create pitch. - Practice playing Vietnamese songs: Con chim ri, Vào rừng hoa, Vietnamese folk songs (Cò lả or Lý cây đa), ancient music (Xàng xê, Mã vũ - Hue music). Foreign song: Auld Lang Syne (Scottish folk song) or Bóng ai qua thềm. * Apply IT in the course: a) Provide online resources: web addresses, traditional music clips. b) Use online resources: selective use of online materials and instructing students on finding information. * Soft skills & Personal development: - Improve perseverance and confidence. - Develop teamwork and independent work skills through assigned tasks. |

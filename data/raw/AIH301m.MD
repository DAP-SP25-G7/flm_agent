## Learning outcomes

| CLO Name | CLO Details | LO Details                                                                                                                            |
|----------|-------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1        | CLO1        | Learners should be able to describe the structure of healthcare systems, AI, and machine learning.                                    |
| 2        | CLO2        | The major challenges and the issues in efforts to improve healthcare delivery and system.                                             |
| 3        | CLO3        | How to apply a framework for medical data mining, makes good research questions, and construct a data mining workflow.                |
| 4        | CLO4        | Define important relationships between the fields of machine learning, biostatistics, and computer programming.                       |
| 5        | CLO5        | The machine learning models in dynamic medical practices and discontinuous timelines impact clinical machine learning applications.   |
| 6        | CLO6        | Principles and practical considerations for integrating AI into clinical workflows, improving the quality of healthcare applications. |
| 7        | CLO7        | Learners should be able to understand AI-emerging technologies in the fields of healthcare.                                           |
| 8        | CLO8        | The ethical issues for the combination of healthcare system and AI.                                                                   |
